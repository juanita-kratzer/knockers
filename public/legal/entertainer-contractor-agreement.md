# Entertainer Contractor Agreement

**Document version:** v1  
**Last updated:** February 2025  
**Status:** Effective.

---

This agreement is between Knockers ("Platform", "we", "us") and you ("Contractor", "Entertainer") in connection with your use of the Knockers platform to offer entertainment services to clients.

---

## 1. Independent contractor status

1.1 You are an **independent contractor**, not an employee, agent, or partner of the Platform. You are responsible for your own tax, superannuation, insurance, and regulatory compliance in connection with your entertainment services.

1.2 **No agency relationship.** We do not direct or control how you perform your services (except for platform rules and safety requirements). You decide when to accept bookings, what services to offer, and how to perform them, within the bounds of this agreement and our policies.

---

## 2. Payment structure

2.1 Clients pay through the platform. We collect fees (including platform fee) and remit your share in accordance with our payment and payout terms (e.g. after booking completion, subject to holds or disputes).

2.2 **Platform fee:** A fee per booking is retained by the Platform as disclosed in-app. This is non-refundable except as stated in the cancellation policy.

2.3 You are responsible for any tax on your earnings and for keeping records. We may provide tax information (e.g. annual statements) where required by law.

---

## 3. Cancellation and no-show

3.1 **Cancellation penalties** apply as set out in the platform cancellation policy (see policy configuration). Cancelling accepted bookings may result in fees or penalties. Repeated cancellations or no-shows may lead to suspension or termination of your access.

3.2 You must communicate promptly with the client and the platform if you cannot fulfil a booking.

---

## 4. Code of conduct

4.1 You will:

- Provide services in a professional and lawful manner.
- Honour accepted bookings unless cancellation is permitted under the policy.
- Respect client privacy and event details.
- Use platform communication and payment tools as required; not circumvent the platform for paid work arising from it (where prohibited by policy).
- Cooperate with verification and safety processes.

4.2 You will not:

- Offer or provide illegal services, nudity, sexual services, or "full service" of a sexual nature.
- Harass, discriminate against, or abuse clients or other users.
- Misrepresent your identity, qualifications, or services.
- Breach any applicable law or third-party rights in connection with your use of the platform.

---

## 5. Prohibited conduct

5.1 **Illegal services, nudity, explicit acts:** Any offer or provision of illegal services, nudity, or sexually explicit services is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate termination and may be reported to authorities.

5.2 You must comply with all platform policies, including those on contact sharing, content, and safety.

---

## 6. Review system

6.1 You acknowledge that clients may leave reviews and ratings after a booking. We may display these on the platform and use them to improve the service and enforce policies. We do not guarantee removal of reviews except where they violate our policies or law.

---

## 7. Strikes and suspension

7.1 We may operate a strike or warning system for policy breaches (e.g. cancellations, no-shows, misconduct). Serious or repeated breaches may result in suspension or permanent removal from the platform. You agree to these consequences as set out in our current policies.

---

## 8. Liability for group events (client responsibility)

8.1 For group events, the **client** is responsible for the behaviour of their guests and for ensuring the environment is safe and compliant with this agreement. You may have additional rights or obligations in relation to group events as set out in our policies.

---

## 9. Verification

9.1 You agree to complete any identity, age, or other verification processes we require (e.g. ID upload, work rights). Failure to complete or pass verification may result in suspension or termination of your account.

---

## 10. Dispute resolution

10.1 Disputes between you and the Platform in connection with this agreement will be resolved in accordance with our dispute resolution process and applicable law. We may require an internal process before escalation to courts or tribunals.

---

## 11. Termination

11.1 Either party may end this agreement in accordance with the platform Terms & Conditions. We may suspend or terminate your access immediately for breach of this agreement or our policies.

---

## 12. Entire agreement

12.1 This agreement, together with the Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy, and any other policies we notify you of, sets out the entire agreement between you and the Platform regarding your use of Knockers as an entertainer.

### 1.2 Identity verification (if implemented)

- **ID verification data** — Type of ID selected (e.g. driver's licence, passport), and any documents or images you upload for age or identity verification. Such data is stored securely and used only for verification and compliance purposes.

### 1.3 Communications and content

- **Chat messages** — Messages sent and received through the platform's in-app chat in connection with bookings. We store these to provide the service, support dispute resolution, and where necessary for safety or legal reasons.

### 1.4 Location (if shared)

### 1.5 Payment and transaction

- **Payment metadata** — Transaction amounts, status, and identifiers. We do **not** store full card numbers or CVV; card processing is handled by our payment provider (Stripe). We may store last four digits, brand, and expiry for display purposes where applicable.

## 2. How data is stored

- **Primary storage:** User and booking-related data (including chat) are stored in **Firebase** (Firestore and related services). Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth.
- **Payment data:** Payment and payout data are processed and stored by **Stripe** in accordance with their policies and applicable regulations. We receive and store only the payment metadata necessary to operate the platform (e.g. payment status, amounts, payout records).
- **Security:** We use industry-standard security measures (e.g. encryption in transit and at rest, access controls) to protect your data. No system is completely secure; we encourage you to use a strong password and protect your account.

- **Service providers** — Firebase and Stripe (and any other processors we name in an updated policy) process data on our instructions under agreements that require them to protect your data.

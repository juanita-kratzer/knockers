This document sets out key terms that apply when you ("User", "Client") make or hold a booking through the Knockers platform. By requesting or confirming a booking, you agree to these terms in addition to our Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Booking responsibility

1.1 You are responsible for the accuracy of the booking details you provide (date, time, location, type of event, number of guests, and any special requirements). Incorrect or misleading information may result in cancellation, fees, or suspension as set out in our policies.

1.2 Once a booking is accepted and (where required) deposit paid, you are bound by the platform cancellation policy and the entertainer’s stated rules.

---

## 2. Liability for guests (group events)

2.1 For group events, **you are liable for the behaviour of your guests**. You must ensure that guests do not harass the entertainer, breach platform rules, or create an unsafe environment. Violations may be treated as your responsibility and may result in fees, suspension, or legal action.

---

## 3. Misrepresentation

3.1 **Misrepresentation consequences:** If you provide false or misleading information (e.g. about the nature of the event, number of guests, venue, or your identity), we may cancel the booking, withhold refunds, charge fees, and/or suspend or terminate your account. The entertainer may also have claims against you.

---

## 4. ID and age requirements for group events

4.1 We or the entertainer may require proof of age or identity for you or for guests at group events, where permitted by our policies and law. You agree to comply with any such requirements. Failure to do so may result in cancellation without refund.

---

## 5. Cancellation

5.1 Our **cancellation structure** (timeframes, fees, refunds) is set out in the platform cancellation policy and in-app at the time of booking. You are responsible for reading and understanding it. Cancelling outside the allowed window or in breach of policy may result in charges as specified.

---

## 6. Arrival code and confirmation

6.1 Where the platform uses an **arrival code** or similar confirmation system, you must follow the process (e.g. generate or share the code as instructed) so that the entertainer can confirm arrival and the booking can proceed. Misuse or sharing of codes may result in cancellation or suspension.

---

## 7. Reviews

7.1 **Review system rules:** You may be able to leave a review and rating after a completed booking. Reviews must be honest and not abusive, defamatory, or in breach of our policies. We may remove reviews that violate our rules or the law.

---

## 8. Safety expectations

8.1 You must provide a safe and appropriate environment for the entertainer and comply with any safety guidelines we or the entertainer set. You must not put the entertainer at risk or require them to do anything illegal or outside the agreed services.

---

## 9. Prohibited behaviour

9.1 You must not:

- Harass, threaten, or abuse the entertainer or any guest.
- Request or facilitate illegal services, nudity, or sexual services.
- Record (photo, video, or audio) the entertainer without their explicit consent.
- Share the entertainer’s personal contact details without their consent, or circumvent the platform for payment or communication in breach of our policies.

Violations may result in immediate cancellation, fees, and permanent ban.

---

## 10. Payment obligations

10.1 You must pay all amounts due for the booking (deposit, balance, platform fee) in accordance with the platform flow and the entertainer’s quoted price. Failure to pay may result in cancellation, suspension, and/or collection action. Chargebacks used improperly may result in account termination.

---

## 11. Contact

11.1 For questions about bookings or this agreement, contact us via the details in our Terms & Conditions or in-app support.

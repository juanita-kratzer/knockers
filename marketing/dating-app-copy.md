# Dating app bio / message copy

Use in bios or in first messages where appropriate. Keep it subtle and tasteful.

## Bio one-liners (pick one or adapt)

- "I work with Knockers—the app that connects entertainers and clients. Safety and discretion first."
- "Book me through Knockers for events and parties. Everything’s in the app—deposits, payments, no fuss."
- "Entertainer on Knockers. Professional bookings only. Link in bio / DM for details."

## First-message style (if they ask what you do)

"I'm an entertainer and I use an app called Knockers for private events and parties. It keeps everything professional—bookings, deposits, payments—and they’re big on safety and discretion. If you're ever looking for someone for an event, that’s the place to go."

## Don’t

- Use explicit or suggestive language that violates platform rules.
- Share other people’s profiles or contact details.
- Promise specific services; keep it to "entertainment for events."

## Tone

- Professional, premium, discreet.
- Safety and convenience as the main hooks.

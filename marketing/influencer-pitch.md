# Influencer partnership pitch

Use when reaching out to influencers or creators who might promote Knockers.

## Value proposition

- **For their audience:** A safe, discreet way to book entertainers for events (or to get booked as talent). Premium, professional, app-based.
- **For them:** Affiliate/referral link, potential paid partnership, or event collaboration. We can attribute signups and conversions to their campaign.

## Key points to include

1. **Safety & discretion:** We take both seriously; no sketchy vibes, verified users and traceable bookings.
2. **Professionalism:** Platform for serious entertainers and clients—deposits, payments, and messaging in one place.
3. **Premium positioning:** We're not a generic directory; we're a managed booking experience.
4. **Convenience:** One app for discovery, booking, payment, and communication.

## Email/DM structure

- Short intro (who we are, what Knockers is).
- Why their audience might care (safety, discretion, convenience).
- What we're offering (referral link, campaign code, or partnership terms).
- Clear CTA: "Reply if you'd like a unique link or campaign code to share."

## Campaign tracking

- Provide a unique `campaign` code or ref link so we can track signups and attribute them in the admin dashboard.

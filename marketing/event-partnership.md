# Event partnership outreach

Use when approaching event organisers, venues, or festivals for lead-gen or co-promotion.

## Pitch angle

- **For the event:** We can help their attendees or performers find/book entertainers (or get booked) through a safe, professional platform.
- **For us:** Lead capture (with consent), QR codes or unique links, campaign tracking.

## Key messages

- **Safety:** "We verify users and keep everything in-app—deposits, payments, messaging. Discretion and safety are core."
- **Professionalism:** "Knockers is for serious entertainers and clients. No grey areas—clear terms, clear payments."
- **Premium:** "We position this as a premium booking experience, not a generic directory."
- **Convenience:** "One app for discovery, booking, and payment. Great for busy event crowds."

## What we can offer

- Unique campaign code or QR code for the event.
- Optional lead capture (name, phone, email) with consent and marketing opt-in.
- Attribution in our admin dashboard (campaignId, source = event).

## Ask

- "Can we have a small presence (flyer, QR code, sign) with a unique link/code so we can track signups from your event?"
- "Would you share our link with performers or attendees who might want to join as entertainers or book for future events?"

## Consent

- Any lead capture must include clear opt-in for marketing (SMS/email) and storage in our leads CRM. Export only opt-in leads for outreach.

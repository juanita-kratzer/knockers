# Street outreach script

Use when approaching potential entertainers or clients in person.

## Key messages

- **Safety first:** "We're a platform that puts entertainer safety and discretion first. All bookings are verified and traceable."
- **Professionalism:** "Knockers is for serious entertainers and clients. No pressure, no sketchy vibes—just a clean way to book and get booked."
- **Premium service:** "We help entertainers set their own rates, manage deposits, and get paid securely. For clients, it's one place to find vetted talent."
- **Convenience:** "Everything happens in the app: messaging, deposits, arrival codes. You're in control."

## Opening (30 seconds)

"Hey, do you do private events or parties? We run an app called Knockers that connects entertainers with clients—everything's above board, deposits and payments in the app, and we care a lot about safety and discretion. If you're interested I can send you a link or we can grab your number and have someone follow up."

## If they're interested

- Get name + phone (and email if easy).
- Note: street, location, and any specific interest (entertainer vs client).
- Say: "We'll send you a link to sign up. You can look around with no obligation."

## Don't

- Promise specific earnings or bookings.
- Take payment on the street.
- Share other people's details.

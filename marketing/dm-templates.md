# DM templates (social / in-app)

Use for Instagram, TikTok DMs or in-app outreach. Keep short and respectful.

## For potential entertainers

**Opener (cold):**
"Hi! I came across your profile and think you'd fit really well on Knockers—we're a platform that connects entertainers with clients for private events. Everything is app-based (bookings, deposits, payments) and we prioritise safety and discretion. Happy to send a link if you'd like to check it out. No pressure."

**Follow-up (if they asked for more info):**
"Here’s the link: [REF_LINK]. You can sign up as an entertainer and set your own rates. We handle deposits and payouts in the app. If you have any questions, reply here or contact us through the app."

## For potential clients

**Opener:**
"Hi! We run Knockers—a booking platform for entertainers for private events and parties. All entertainers are vetted, and bookings are done through the app (deposits, messaging, payments). If you ever need someone for an event, we’d love to help. Here’s the link: [SITE_LINK]."

## Tone

- Safety, discretion, and professionalism in every message.
- No pressure; "no obligation" and "check it out" work well.
- Never share other users’ details or promise specific outcomes.

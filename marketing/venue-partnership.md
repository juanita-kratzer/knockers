# Venue partnership outreach

Use when approaching venues (bars, clubs, private spaces) for referrals or co-promotion.

## Value for the venue

- **For their clients:** A trusted way to book entertainers for private events (e.g. in a VIP area or for a party).
- **For entertainers:** A channel to get in front of venue clients who might book them.
- **For the venue:** Potential kickback, referral fee, or simply better service for their guests.

## Key messages

- **Safety & discretion:** "All bookings go through our app—verified users, deposits, and payments. We take safety and discretion seriously."
- **Professionalism:** "Knockers is a premium booking platform. No grey areas; everything is traceable and above board."
- **Convenience:** "Your guests can discover, book, and pay in one app. We handle the logistics."

## What we can offer

- Unique campaign or referral code for the venue.
- Attribution in our system (campaignId, source = venue).
- Optional co-branded materials (QR code, small sign) for "Book entertainment via Knockers."

## Ask

- "Can we place a QR code or link at the bar or in VIP so guests can book entertainers through us? We’ll attribute all signups to your venue."
- "Would you refer entertainers who perform here to join Knockers? We can give you a unique link so we track referrals."

## Tone

- Premium, professional, discretion-first.
- Frame as a service upgrade for the venue and their clients.

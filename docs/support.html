Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download Knockers from the App Store and sign up with your email. You'll be guided through identity verification to keep the platform safe.

What is the verification fee?

Entertainers pay a one-time $1.99 verification fee to go live on the platform. This helps us maintain a trusted community.

How do I get a Police Check Verified badge?

After signing up, you can upload your police check. Once reviewed and approved, a verified badge will appear on your profile.

How do I request a refund?

For in-app purchases, you can manage your subscription through the app's Customer Center or contact Apple Support directly.
